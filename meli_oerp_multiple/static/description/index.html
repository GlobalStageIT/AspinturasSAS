MercadoLibre Multiple / MercadoLibre Publisher Extension

Extension for having Multiple Accounts per Company and Multiple Publications per Product / Connector for Odoo.

What it is?

MercadoLibre Multiple (meli_oerp_multiple) by Moldeo Interactive is a MercadoLibre Extension for MercadoLibre Publisher Connector for Odoo.
This is the premium extension to give more than an account to your companies, and also associate or bind any product to multiple publications.

This suite is composed by and depends on:

  • MercadoLibre Publisher
  • MercadoLibre Stock Extension
  • MercadoLibre Accounting Extension

Features

MercadoLibre Multiple Features

  • Multiple Accounts
    • Infinite Accounts for each company
    • Shared Account Configurations
    • Account specific overview
  • Multiple Publications
    • Infinite publications for each product
    • Associations/Bindings can share differents accounts for the same product
Multiple accounts for each company
Multiple publications for each product

  • Massive publication from Odoo to MercadoLibre / Import all products from MercadoLibre to Odoo

    • Variations with all attributes combinations
    • SKU options
  • Get Orders, Clients and Shipping information from MercadoLibre to Odoo
    • Import pack orders
    • Import fee cost and shipping cost
    • Use SKU to find existent Product
  • Massive and automatic synchronization
    • Stock and Prices
    • Realtime for orders using mercadolibre notifications
